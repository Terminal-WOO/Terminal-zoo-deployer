# Culture Principles - DevOps en Team Topologies

Dit document beschrijft de culturele principes voor het App Store platform team, gebaseerd op DevOps en Team Topologies.

## Overzicht

Een collaborative culture is essentieel voor platform success. Dit document beschrijft de culturele principes die het platform team volgt.

## DevOps Principes

### 1. Collaboration over Silos

**Principle**: Cross-functional collaboration in plaats van siloed teams.

**Practices**:
- Cross-functional teams
- Shared ownership
- Joint planning
- Knowledge sharing

**Benefits**:
- Faster delivery
- Better quality
- Improved communication
- Reduced friction

---

### 2. Shared Responsibility

**Principle**: Iedereen is verantwoordelijk voor platform success.

**Practices**:
- Shared on-call
- Blameless post-mortems
- Collective ownership
- Team accountability

**Benefits**:
- Better reliability
- Faster incident response
- Improved learning
- Stronger team

---

### 3. Continuous Improvement

**Principle**: Platform evolueert continu op basis van feedback en learning.

**Practices**:
- Regular retrospectives
- Experimentation
- Learning culture
- Incremental improvements

**Benefits**:
- Better platform
- Improved processes
- Team growth
- Innovation

---

### 4. Learning Culture

**Principle**: Fouten zijn learning opportunities, niet failures.

**Practices**:
- Blameless post-mortems
- Knowledge sharing
- Experimentation
- Continuous learning

**Benefits**:
- Better reliability
- Faster learning
- Innovation
- Team growth

---

## Team Topologies

### Platform Team

**Type**: Platform Team (volgens Team Topologies)

**Purpose**: Provides platform capabilities aan development teams.

**Responsibilities**:
- Platform development
- Platform maintenance
- Developer support
- Platform evolution

**Interaction Mode**: 
- **Facilitating**: Helps development teams
- **Enabling**: Provides capabilities
- **Collaborating**: Works with teams

---

### Development Teams

**Type**: Stream-Aligned Teams

**Purpose**: Deliver business value via applications.

**Interaction with Platform**:
- Use platform capabilities
- Provide feedback
- Contribute improvements
- Report issues

---

### Collaboration Patterns

**Platform ↔ Development Teams**:
- Regular sync meetings
- Joint planning
- Shared ownership
- Continuous feedback

**Platform ↔ Business**:
- Roadmap alignment
- Value communication
- Business metrics
- Strategic planning
